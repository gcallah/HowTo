Hosting a Class at PythonAnywhere

PythonAnywhere provides hosting services that, while focused on Python, certainly do not end there: one can also run shell, C, C++, awk, and Ruby programs with an account (as well as compiling C and C++ programs).

The relevance of this service to those who teach programming courses is that PA has special education services. Students can get free accounts, but even better than that, PA will create these accounts for you, and, even better, student accounts allow another account to be set as the instructor, and the instructor can log in and see the students work.

When I used this service for my OS class, I simply sent PA a list of students Net IDs (to use as their login names) along with an account name for the TA who would be their supervisor. Once that was done, I could assign C coding, with no worries about setting up the compiler, getting mailed source code or executables, or complaints like "But it ran on my laptop!"

More info on PythonAnywhere's education page.
