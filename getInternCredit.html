Get Credit for Your Internship

NYU Tandon offers the possibility of credit in your major for internships. Here is a link to the bulletin.

Performance assessment will be done once at midterm (advisory, S/U) and again at the completion of the semester (P/F). The grade will be based on: three reports on reflection and documentation of activities.

  1. At the start of the semester: report on individual goals for the co-op/internship
  2. Mid-semester: work report on tasks completed through the midterm
  3. End-of-semester: evaluation of work experience final paper that reflects on disciplinary and professional skills acquired during the work experience. Professional skills include teamwork, leadership skills, ethics, presentation, writing, time management, and project management.
